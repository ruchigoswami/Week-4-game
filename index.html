Crystals Collector
Rules
  1. You will be given a random number of crystals at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By cliking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to random number,you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it
  5. Each time when game will starts,the game will change the values of each crystal.
